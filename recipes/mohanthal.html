Mohan Thal

Description:

Mohan Thal is a traditional South Indian sweet dish that is made with gram flour, semolina, and ghee.

Ingriendents:

  • 1 cup gram flour (besan)
  • 1/2 cup semolina (rava)
  • 1/2 cup ghee
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • 2 tablespoons ghee
  • 1/4 cup chopped cashews
  • 1/4 cup raisins
