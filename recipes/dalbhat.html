Dal Bhat

Description:

Dal Bhat is a traditional Indian dish that consists of steamed rice(Bhat) and lentil soup(Dal).

Ingredients:

    For the Dal:

  • 1 cup lentils (you can use masoor dal, moong dal, or toor dal)
  • 4 cups water
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 inch piece of ginger, minced
  • 2 medium tomatoes, finely chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon garam masala powder
  • 2 tablespoons chopped cilantro

    For the Bhat (rice):

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 2 cups water
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Steps To make a Dal Bhat:

  1. Wash and soak the lentils for at least 30 mins.
  2. Then, In a pot, cook the lentils with water, turmeric, and salt until they are soft and mushy.
  3. In a sepearate pan, heat oil and add cumin seeds, mustard seeds, and chopped onions.
  4. Once the onions are golden brown, add chopped garlic, ginger, and tomatoes and cook until the tomatoes are soft.
  5. Add the cooked lentils to the pan and simmer for a few minutes.
  6. Serve the Dal with steamed rice and a side of pickled vegetables or chuteny.
